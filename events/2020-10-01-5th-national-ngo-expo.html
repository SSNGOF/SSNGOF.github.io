NNGO EXPO: Radio Miraya Breakfast Show 1 October 2020

The NGO Forum is proud to announce the fifth 5th National NGO Expo that will take place on the 29th to 31st October 2020 in Juba. The 2020 Expo has the theme: National NGOs for Resilience, Stability and Innovative Development.

NNGO Expo Mission

The NNGO Expo is a platform for National NGOs to showcase their activities and diversity to the nation, Government, international actors and business community. All are welcome to attend!

COVID-19

The 2020 NNGO Expo will be organised in compliance with recommended COVID-19 preventive measures – social distancing, compulsory hand washing and wearing of face masks.

More Info

The NGO Forum will communicate further details about the NNGO Expo in the course of the coming weeks. Follow us to get more information about the Expo as we move closer to the big event!
